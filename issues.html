NVR and Cameras

You can enter to your NVR but do not visualize or see(whatch) your cameras streaming

This could happen when there's not a streaming port or when the streaming port being use for another
device in the network.
To solve this check if there's a (RSTP)streaming port like 554 in the NVR or change it to the 8554 which also works.
